## Sources checked and NOT used (dry, honestly reported)

- **Deaf-owned Buffalo storefronts:** Gallaudet WNY guide, Deafopia, deaffriendly, deafplaces — nothing new in Erie County (Golden Harvest Bakery: Rochester, closed 2022).
- **Non-veteran disabled-owned storefronts** via Buffalo press (Buffalo News/WGRZ/Buffalo Rising): no citable first-party "disabled-owned" statements found. No Disability:IN DOBE-certified visitable Erie County businesses found. This category still needs WNYIL partner outreach + owner self-attestation, not scraping (unchanged from batch 1).
- **Wheel the World** has measured profiles for Buffalo RiverWorks and West Side Bazaar, but pages are JS-rendered and unfetchable here — browser-based follow-up candidate.
- **parks.ny.gov park pages** (Beaver Island, Knox Farm, Woodlawn Beach, Evangola): no explicit accessibility sections → omitted rather than guessed. **Delaware Park** (bfloparks.org): no accessibility features stated → omitted.
- **Hotels:** nothing beyond generic booking-engine ADA text → skipped.
- **Big Ditch Brewing:** the widely-repeated "second floor not accessible" claim exists only in UGC/third-party sources → omitted entirely.
